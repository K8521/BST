How to use

  • Insert/Delete/Search integers. Duplicates are ignored.
  • Select traversal, then Traverse or Animate.
  • Random creates a random tree; Balanced creates a balanced one.
  • Shortcuts: Ctrl+I Insert, Delete key Delete, Ctrl+F Search, Ctrl+L Clear.
